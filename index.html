EGA Management and Financial Solutions is a multidisciplinary consulting firm with a strong background in both large, established organizations and new business ventures. The firm leverages over 25 years of professional experience in Ethiopia and abroad to provide corporate management and financial solutions to a wide range of clients, including private and public companies, government institutions, bilateral organizations, and charities. EGA also collaborates with international affiliates in the USA, Kenya, and Uganda on global assignments and projects.

EGA is committed to a hands-on approach, ensuring a firm manager is always in control of key project functions. The company emphasizes teamwork and clear communication among all stakeholders to ensure success on every project. Equipped with the latest technology, professional staff, and up-to-date Professional Indemnity Insurance, EGA is prepared to deliver high-quality professional services for any assignment, regardless of its size or location.
