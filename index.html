Core Sectors

Yadah Gemstone PLC is a dynamic and forward-thinking company established in 2017 with a clear vision to make a significant difference in Ethiopia's vital agriculture and mining sectors. Founded by a team of experienced professionals with a combined 26 years of expertise across diverse industries, our company is committed to sustainable growth, innovation, and contributing to the economic development of the nation.

Our mission is to be a leader in the mining and agriculture sectors, making a tangible positive impact on the Ethiopian economy through sustainable practices and superior quality products. We aim to diversify our portfolio and leverage our expertise in construction and water works to support broader national development goals.

At Yadah Gemstone PLC, we are dedicated to operating with the highest ethical standards. Our commitment extends beyond business success to include the well-being of the environment and the communities in which we operate. We employ sustainable mining and agricultural practices to minimize our ecological footprint and actively engage with local communities to ensure our operations contribute positively to their development.

Core Business Sectors

We operate across four key sectors, bringing expertise and innovation to Ethiopia's economic development.

Yadah Gemstone PLC is a primary producer and exporter of high-quality minerals. We own and operate mining sites in the Southern, Southwestern, and Eastern Oromia regions, as well as Dire Dawa. Our operations focus on the ethical and efficient extraction of a variety of precious and industrial minerals for the export market.

Minerals Mined:

  • Gemstone
  • Gold
  • Copper (CU)
  • Coal
  • Lithium
  • Tantalum
  • Niobium

Our agricultural division manages a vast farm of over 200 hectares in the southwest region of Ethiopia. We specialize in the cultivation of a wide range of export-quality cereal crops and spices, contributing to both local food security and international trade. We also maintain a key collaboration with TBA Trading PLC to enhance our operations and market reach.

Crops & Spices:

  • Sesame
  • Mung Beans
  • Kidney Beans
  • Chickpea
  • Navy Beans
  • Soya Beans
  • Korarima
  • Cloves
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Coffee Beans

With over a decade of experience in the construction industry, our team brings proven expertise to the development of critical infrastructure. Our portfolio includes a focus on building and road construction projects, showcasing our commitment to modern and durable engineering practices. We are actively engaged in this emerging sector, providing reliable and high-quality services.

Our water works division is dedicated to providing essential services for water resource management. We offer comprehensive solutions that include professional water drilling, design, and supervision. Our commitment to excellence in this sector helps support communities and industries with reliable access to water resources.
